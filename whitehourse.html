    SKATEBOARD SPOT APP DESIGN

    For Web Design Class

    This peoject is about a subculture - Skateboard. Skateboard visual language is very diverse, I choice the most tipical one "Graffiti". Graffiti is the most important part in skateboard, it contain rebelity and the desire of freedom. This App is a spot app, which provide imformation of skateboarding spot. It allows skateboarder rate and comment for every spot. Or skateboarder can submit new skateboarding spot. And skateboarder can communicate through this app.
