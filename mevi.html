    ME VI

    VI design

    Additional Details：This VI base on a doll named "ME". The doll is my final project as an undergraduate. Because my paper title is “Rethink Concerning the Purpose of Design”, my works intend to keep away from the trend of formal beauty which people have been blindly pursuing. Therefore, I designed a doll of distinct appearance based on the process of self-analysis, and we call this doll “me”. Shortly after, the doll has been bought by a design company named "MEGIC EYE" which capital letters are "ME". They think our design conforms to the spirit of their company. As they requested, I designed a set of visual identity (VI) items for their company according to the image of the doll. I selected the shape of the doll’s head and highlighted the eyes to echo the name of their company.
