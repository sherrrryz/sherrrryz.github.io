Cubisim Zine

Publication Design



Cubism was one of the rst truly modern movements in art. It evolved during a period between Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque. Juan Gris signi cantly developed the style. The principles of Cubism are geometricity, multiple views and passage. Cubism tried to describe in visual terms of the Fourth Dimension. When I created my zine, I focused on showing the geometric and multiple views of cubism. For the mediums, I used collages, cut paper, spray paint, and photography. The reason I chose this mediums is that collage was one of the skills established during the Cubist move- ment involving cutting pieces of paper items.
