    TO MARS - A SCIENCE FICTION

    Typography work

    This book is a science fiction, it started in 1946. I collected 15 texts and 15 poems, and arranged all the text based on timeline. The color of pages changes when the places in the story changes. For example, when the story happen on Mars, paper will be yellow. And page size changes when timeline changes. There are 2 type of timeline in this book, when the story be told based on Sol-timeline, pages become shorter. I used "Mostra Nuova" as main typeface. There are 6 columns in this book, so that the system could be more flexible.
